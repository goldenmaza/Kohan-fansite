The Shadow race

The Shadow race contain creatures that are very befouled, they want nothing for themselves and they only serve one master: Ahriman. Their numbers are what makes them dangerous, and of course their morale.

Their biggest weakness are from Khaldunite attacks. With a 20% extra damage, per strike, it makes them weak against an army of anything that uses that kind of weaponry or magic.

One strong suite that this race has are their well guarded forts that houses a horde of Reavers, these as well as the Gauri forts, are two of the strongest in the game.
